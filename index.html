What exchanges and markets does QuantumTrade Pro support?

QuantumTrade Pro currently supports 28 major cryptocurrency exchanges including Binance, Coinbase Pro, Kraken, Bitfinex, and Deribit. For traditional markets, we integrate with 12 major brokers offering forex, commodities, and indices. The platform also supports CME Group futures through our institutional partners. We're continuously expanding our exchange coverage based on liquidity and user demand.

How does the adaptive risk management system work?

Our risk system employs multiple layers of protection: 1) Real-time volatility measurement adjusts position sizes dynamically, 2) Correlation matrices identify concentrated exposures across your portfolio, 3) Market regime detection switches between conservative and aggressive strategies, and 4) Circuit breakers automatically pause trading during extreme events. You maintain full control over risk parameters while benefiting from our institutional-grade safeguards.

What kind of hardware do I need for optimal performance?

For basic operation, QuantumTrade Pro runs well on any modern Windows PC meeting the minimum requirements. For high-frequency trading or complex strategies, we recommend: Intel i7/i9 or AMD Ryzen 7/9 processor, 32GB RAM, NVIDIA RTX 3060+ GPU, and NVMe SSD storage. The software automatically scales its computational load based on available hardware. Our colocated server option provides institutional-grade infrastructure for demanding users.

Can I backtest my strategies with historical data?

Yes, QuantumTrade Pro includes our QuantumBacktest engine with: 1) 7+ years of tick-level historical data for all supported markets, 2) Realistic spread and slippage modeling, 3) Monte Carlo simulation for robustness testing, and 4) Walk-forward optimization. Our backtesting environment accounts for exchange-specific nuances like fee schedules and order book dynamics to provide accurate performance estimates.
